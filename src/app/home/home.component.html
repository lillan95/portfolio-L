Who am I?

I am a happy and energetic 23 year old who’s currently studying the Media Technology masters at KTH. I have always loved visual creativity and been painting and created sculptures since I was young. Because of this I have gained quite a bit of knowledge in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash and Indesign. I have also forayed into 3D design with applications such as Zbrush and Maya. Eventually I started with CSS, and that is what started me on the path that led me to programming and the Media Technology program at KTH.

What do I do?

At KTH I have taken courses that have given me great fundamentals in Python, Matlab, HTML, JavaScript, PHP, XML, and CSS. To find a flow for my creativity I joined the student union newspaper in the fall of 2015 and I have been working as an illustrator and photographer for both the print and web editions. My proudest achievements are the cover of the first edition of OL 15/16 that was published in November of 2015, which reached a circulation of 16 000 students, and the exhibition of Puzzle Tower VR at the technical museum in Stockholm in autumn of 2018.
