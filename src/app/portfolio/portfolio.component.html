Puzzle Tower VR 2017-12-01

In a group of 6 people, we developed the VR game for the game design course given at KTH, Stockholm. I was the lead graphics designer during the project but I also helped . It was developed in Unity and is used with HTC Vive. Since October 2018 the game is included in the exhibition "Play beyond play" at the Tekniska museet in Stockholm.
